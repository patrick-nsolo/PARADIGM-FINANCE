FUND MANAGEMENT

Reserve Funds

A helpful way to earn high returns on idle funds. This is suitable for investors who receive salary income or fixed wages/allowance and wish to grow the money each pay-day.

Benefits

  • Preserve your asset
  • Quarterly interest payment option
  • Flexible entry and exit
  • Interest of 10% p.a. revolving average

Features

  • N50,000 minimum investment
  • N10,000 minimum additional investment
  • Tenor - 24 months

i-Cash Fund

A rewarding way to save and get higher interest rates than a traditional savings account. Suitable for investors who have cash in their current and savings account and wish to earn higher-returns.

Benefits

Features

  • Tenor - 24 months
