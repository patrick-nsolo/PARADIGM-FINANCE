About Us

Paradigm Finance at a glance

Paradigm Finance is a digitally enabled finance company,licensed in 2017 by the Central Bank Of Nigeria(CBN) to practice in Nigeria. We provide finance to mass and middle-income employees and structured businesses in selected markets and have witnessed steady business growth over the years.

Our Vision

To become a finance company of repute and dependability with presence in at least 3 regional zones of Nigeria in 5 years(2027), using a fairly motivated work force driven by technology and give satisfaction to our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as the most dependable funding option for our clients. We want to be acknowledged for the quality of our people and the excellent returns we provide our investors.
